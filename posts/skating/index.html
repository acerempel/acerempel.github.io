Skating

While skating at the Oval one Sunday afternoon with a few friends, I felt compelled to offer an explanation for why I was alternating smooth skating with bouts of stumbling: “Sometimes I space out for a moment and suddenly I'm no longer in the zone! But it's okay, I just take a moment to center myself and get back in the groove.” One of my friends thought this remark expressed something about my essence. That's probably true. Though I now think that my real problem was that my skates were not laced tightly enough.
