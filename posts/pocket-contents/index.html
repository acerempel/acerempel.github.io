What do I have in my pock­ets?

A beeswax ta­per—
A wrap­per from a Hershey kiss—
A phone—
A pen—
A tis­sue—
A record­ing de­vice (borrowed from a friend)—
A note­book—
A uni­ver­sity ID card—
A debit card—
A house key—
Two dol­lars eighty-ﬁve cents—
One of those cards you can peel off of a McDonald’s cof­fee cup where once you have seven stick­ers you get a free drink—
A cork—
A gro­cery re­ceipt—
An ATM re­ceipt—
Another pen—
An al­mond—
A pam­phlet from a re­cent Winter Retreat—
A scrap of pa­per with a poem writ­ten on it (“Great Things Have Happened” by Alden Nolan), and—thank heav­ens!—
A tis­sue.
