Salted tahini chocolate chip cookies

—That is, I baked some! Recipe courtesy of David Lebovitz.

They turned out really well! It's a very soft and sticky dough, and the cookies expanded during baking quite a bit more than I anticipated. The result is not actually such a radically different cookie from the typical chocolate chip than you might suppose;—the tahini just adds a lovely nutty and slightly bitter shade, without changing the texture. I've made this recipe before, and this run only confirms its place of honour in my recipe file.
