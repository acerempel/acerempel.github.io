Introduction

Hello! Welcome!

I don’t really have much to say. The only reason this website exists is that I find it enjoyable to work on it in my spare time (and even in time I have not to spare!).

I tend not to imagine anyone reading what I write here (though I realize this may not be the case), so I post highly irregularly. Posts are dated by when I originally composed their content. This date may be months or years earlier than when the post appears here—I often post backdated items that I had written as a note to myself much earlier.

There are no comments (yet), but if you send me an email commenting on a post, I might (with your permission) post your comment below the post.

I am constantly intermittently adding posts, editing posts, removing posts, adding them back again, and tweaking the design of the site, so check back again soon! (Someday I hope to have an RSS/Atom feed, so you can simply subscribe, if you are old-school like me and use an RSS reader. I have no plans for any kind of social media integration.)

I hope your find this website an amusing stop on your internet travels.

Yours,
Alan
