Introduction

Hello! Welcome to my website!

The only reason this website exists is that I find it enjoyable to work on it in my spare time (and even in time I have not to spare!). I also like experimenting with alternatives to the usual ways of presenting oneself in writing on the Internet. I have nothing against social media, though, and you may find me on Facebook and Instagram and maybe others.

Why “three dots”? Because three dots is the visual form of the ellipsis (‘…’), and I am often an elliptical man.

Concerning the blog: I post highly irregularly. Posts are dated by when I originally composed their content. This date may be months or years earlier than when the post appears here—I often post backdated items that I had written as a note to myself much earlier.

There is no commenting facility (yet), but if you send me an email commenting on a post, I might (with your permission) post your comment below the post.

Concerning how I made this website: I used to write blog posts &c. in Markdown and convert them into webpages using various iterations of programs I wrote for the purpose. Nowadays I just write HTML by hand, which is fun in its own way.

I am constantly intermittently adding, editing, removing stuff, adding stuff back again, and tweaking the design of the site, so check back again soon!

I hope your find this website an amusing stop on your internet travels.

Yours,
Alan
