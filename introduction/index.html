Introduction

Good evening! I am Alan; this is my website. I am a student at the University of King's College (Halifax, NS). Please take up a chair; I will bring you some sparkling water in a moment. Feel free to browse.

Why “three dots”? Because three dots is the visual form of the ellipsis (‘…’), and I am often an elliptical man.

Concerning the blog: I post highly irregularly. Posts are dated by when I originally composed their content. This date may be months or years earlier than when the post appears here—I often post backdated items that I had written as a note to myself much earlier.

I am constantly intermittently adding, editing, removing stuff, adding stuff back again, and tweaking the design of the site, so check back again soon!

I hope your find this website an amusing stop on your internet travels.

Yours,
Alan
