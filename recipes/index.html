Selected recipes

that I have enjoyed making and sharing with friends – mostly from Smitten Kitchen and David Lebovitz.

  • Tahini chocolate chip cookies (see also an associated blog post)
  • French apple cake (note to self: should make this again soon, since I happen to have all the ingredients!)

  • Italian bread, made with a biga. For what is a biga, see the recipe! It is a very delicious and thoroughly fermented bread that takes a long time and a lot of kneading to make.

  • Magic apple & plum cobbler – Jocelyn and I have made this I think almost every time we have had friends over to our apartment, and it has always been met with praise. It is the kind of ingenious recipe that is completely trivial to make and invariably completely delicious.
